Hydra Vulgaris

Biome

Freshwater ponds and lakes

Diet

Carnivore

Trophic level

Secondary consumer

Hydra Vulgaris can reproduce via sexual reproduction, budding, and direct regeneration. Additionally, they have not been observed to undergo senescence, meaning that the cells of the organism are immortal to aging.

The Hydra Vulgaris is capable of sensing prey via a chemical response to Reduced Glutathione, a chemical only present in living animals, despite having no complex sensory organs.
