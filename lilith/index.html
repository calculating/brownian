Arachnanthus Lilith

Biome

Benthic, ocean vent

Diet

Carnivore

Trophic level

Secondary consumer

Arachnanthus Lilith has pale tentacles that help to camouflage it in the sandy ocean floor. It is also thought to be nocturnally active.

The Lilith tube anemone has inner tentacles which help to guide food into its mouth after being traped by the larger tentacles.
